Our Story

Where Beauty Meets Warmth

Located at the heart of Padle Gaon in Thane, Kyra Beauty Salon was established with a simple vision: to create a sanctuary where beauty and self-care are celebrated. We identify as women-owned and are driven by a commitment to exceptional service and a passion for helping our clients look and feel their absolute best.

From the moment you walk in, our friendly and professional staff are dedicated to making you feel comfortable and valued. We maintain the highest standards of hygiene, creating a clean and inviting environment for every client.
